Select an article to brief

The article should identify a management problem (e.g., stunting reducing size structure, population declines, angler satisfaction). In the brief you will need to 1) summarize the article for a lay audience and 2) discuss the management implications for Mississippi Fisheries.

You are not limited to the journals below but they are recommended.

  • North American Journal of Fisheries Management
  • Canadian Journal of Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences
  • Transactions of the American Fisheries Society
  • Fisheries Management and Ecology

Once you identify an article send a pdf copy to me by the due date for full credit.

What should the brief cover?

  1. What was the objectives of the study
  2. What were the relevant methods and results.
  3. What are the management implications for a Mississippi Fishery
    • Specifically you need identify a problem the study may apply to
    • Identify how the study and results can potential solve the problem and metrics that might be monitored to evaluate the problem
